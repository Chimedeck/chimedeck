- `server/extensions/automation/api/create.ts` — `POST /api/v1/boards/:boardId/automations` with validation (automation-type-invalid, trigger-type-unknown, action-type-unknown).
- `server/extensions/automation/api/get.ts` — `GET /api/v1/boards/:boardId/automations/:automationId` with 404 on missing.
- `server/extensions/automation/api/update.ts` — `PATCH /api/v1/boards/:boardId/automations/:automationId` replacing trigger/actions atomically.
- `server/extensions/automation/api/delete.ts` — `DELETE /api/v1/boards/:boardId/automations/:automationId` with cascaded cleanup.
- `server/extensions/automation/api/index.ts` — router wiring all CRUD handlers.
- `server/mods/events/dispatch.ts` — thin wrapper over `writeEvent` that fires `evaluate()` after event persistence.
- `tests/integration/automation/crud.test.ts` — 15 integration tests covering auth guards, validation error codes, and response shapes (all passing).
