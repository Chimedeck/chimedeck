## New
- Added `db/migrations/0092_board_notification_type_preferences.ts`:
  - Creates the `board_notification_type_preferences` table with columns: `id`, `user_id`, `board_id`, `type`, `in_app_enabled` (default true), `email_enabled` (default true), `created_at`, `updated_at`.
  - Enforces a unique constraint on `(user_id, board_id, type)`.
  - Adds a composite index on `(user_id, board_id)`.
  - CHECK constraint on `type` (matching notification types in `0091_notification_types_extend2.ts`).
  - Foreign keys to `users` and `boards` with CASCADE delete.
