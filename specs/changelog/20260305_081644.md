## Update
- Modified `server/extensions/plugins/sdk/jh-instance.ts`: `t.get()` and `t.set()` now auto-resolve `resourceId` from `this.args[scope]?.id` and `boardId` from `this.args.board?.id` before sending `DATA_GET`/`DATA_SET` messages to the host.

## New
- Plugin calls to `t.get()` and `t.set()` no longer require the caller to pass `resourceId` or `boardId` explicitly — both are derived automatically from the context args injected by the host during `CAPABILITY_INVOKE`, eliminating 400 errors caused by missing fields.
