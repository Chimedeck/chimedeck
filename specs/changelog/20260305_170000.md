## New

- Added `APP_ALLOWED_ORIGINS` env support (comma-separated extra board hosts) and exported normalized `allowedBoardOrigins` / `allowedCorsOrigins` from `plugins/sharetribe-horizon-escrow/server/config/app.ts`.
- Documented `APP_ALLOWED_ORIGINS` in `plugins/sharetribe-horizon-escrow/.env.template` and setup notes in `plugins/sharetribe-horizon-escrow/README.md`.
