## New
- Added `db/migrations/0021_plugins.ts`, a migration that creates the four core plugin system tables:
  - `plugins`: Registry of all plugins with metadata, capabilities (jsonb), `is_public`/`is_active` flags, and `api_key`.
  - `board_plugins`: Tracks enabled plugins per board, with `disabled_at` (soft-delete) and a UNIQUE constraint on `(board_id, plugin_id)`.
  - `plugin_data`: Key/value storage for plugin-scoped data, with a UNIQUE constraint on `(plugin_id, scope, resource_id, user_id, key)`.
  - `plugin_auth_tokens`: Reserved for future OAuth/JWT flows, with FKs to `board_plugins` and `users`.
- All table schemas, foreign keys, and constraints match `specs/architecture/plugins.md` and the sample-project reference.
