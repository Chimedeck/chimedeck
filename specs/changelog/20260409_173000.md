## Update
- Added checklist-item conversion endpoint and routing so a checklist item can be converted into a new card in the same list while keeping the checklist item linked to the created card.
- Extended checklist item update API to support per-item assignee and per-item due date updates.
- Extended card copy behavior to preserve checklist item assignee and due date while resetting checklist-to-card links.
- Extended client checklist item data model and optimistic reducers to handle `assigned_member_id`, `due_date`, and `linked_card_id` fields.
- Updated card modal checklist wiring to support new checklist item actions end-to-end.

## New
- `db/migrations/0103_checklist_item_assignment_due_date.ts`
  - Adds `checklist_items.assigned_member_id` (FK to `users.id`, nullable)
  - Adds `checklist_items.due_date` (nullable timestamp)
  - Adds `checklist_items.linked_card_id` (FK to `cards.id`, nullable)
- Checklist item UI actions:
  - Convert to card
  - Assign member
  - Change due date
  - Item actions menu aligned to Trello-style interaction model

- Reminder options in checklist due-date popover are currently visual-only (`None`) and not persisted.
