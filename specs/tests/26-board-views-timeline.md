# 26. Board Views — Timeline

**Prerequisites:** Flow 11 completed. `Test Card 1` has start and due dates.  
**Continues from:** Board view (any view).  
**Ends with:** Timeline view verified; zoom controls used; returned to kanban view.

---

## Steps

1. Click the **Timeline** view icon in the board header view switcher.
   - **Expected:** Board switches to a horizontal timeline (Gantt-style) layout.

2. Verify `Test Card 1` appears as a bar between its start and due dates.

3. Verify `Test Card 2` (no dates) appears in an **Unscheduled** row at the bottom.

4. Click the **Today** button in the toolbar.
   - **Expected:** Timeline scrolls so today's date is visible in the viewport.

5. Click the **Month** zoom button.
   - **Expected:** Timeline zooms to monthly view.

6. Click the **Week** zoom button.
   - **Expected:** Timeline zooms to weekly view.

7. Click the **Day** zoom button.
   - **Expected:** Timeline zooms to daily view.

8. Drag the right edge (resize handle) of the `Test Card 1` bar to extend the due date by 2 days.
   - **Expected:** Due date updates on the card. Bar extends visually.

9. Switch back to **Kanban** view.
   - **Expected:** Board shows lists and cards as before.
